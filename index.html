Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Swiss Citizenship Test ("the App") is developed and operated by Ali ("we", "us", or "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

The App collects minimal data to function properly:

Anonymous Device Identifier: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to generate a random, anonymous identifier for your device. This is not linked to your name, email, or any personal information. It is used solely to sync your trial usage data across device resets to prevent abuse of the free trial.

Study Progress Data: Your quiz answers, scores, bookmarks, and learning progress are stored locally on your device. A limited subset of trial usage data (number of questions and exams used) may be synced to our cloud servers using the anonymous identifier described above.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information. We do not track your browsing activity. We do not use advertising SDKs or share data with advertisers.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium features. Payment is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or billing address.

Data Storage and Security

Your study progress is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. Anonymous trial data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect this data.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Google Firebase: For anonymous authentication and cloud data storage. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Google's privacy policy can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
